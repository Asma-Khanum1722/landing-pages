### Thyroid Function Test
- **Price:** £75
- **Category:** Blood Tests
- **Description:** A detailed analysis of your thyroid hormones to help identify causes of fatigue, weight changes, and mood swings.
- **Details:** Your thyroid gland controls your body's metabolism. If it's underactive (hypothyroidism) or overactive (hyperthyroidism), it can cause a wide range of symptoms including unexplained weight changes, chronic fatigue, and depression. This test measures key thyroid markers (such as TSH, FT3, and FT4) to give a complete picture of how well your thyroid is working.

### Cholesterol Test
- **Price:** £39
- **Category:** Blood Tests
- **Description:** A comprehensive lipid panel measuring good and bad cholesterol levels to assess your cardiovascular risk.
- **Details:** High cholesterol is a major risk factor for heart disease and stroke, yet it usually has no symptoms. A lipid profile measures your total cholesterol, LDL (bad cholesterol), HDL (good cholesterol), and triglycerides. Knowing your numbers allows you to make informed dietary and lifestyle changes to protect your cardiovascular health.

### CBC / Full Blood Count
- **Price:** £45
- **Category:** Blood Tests
- **Description:** A broad screening test checking for anemia, infection, and many other common diseases.
- **Details:** A Complete Blood Count (CBC) evaluates your overall health and detects a wide range of disorders, including anemia, infection, and leukemia. It measures red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets.

### HbA1c/Diabetes Test
- **Price:** £45
- **Category:** Blood Tests
- **Description:** Measure your average blood sugar levels over the past 2-3 months to screen for or monitor diabetes.
- **Details:** The HbA1c test is a blood test that shows your average blood sugar (glucose) levels over the past two to three months. It is used to diagnose type 2 diabetes and prediabetes, and to monitor how well you are managing your diabetes.

### Iron/Ferritin Blood Test
- **Price:** From £35
- **Category:** Blood Tests
- **Description:** Evaluate your body's iron stores to check for deficiency or overload, often causing tiredness or low energy.
- **Details:** Iron is essential for producing red blood cells. Low iron levels can lead to anemia, causing fatigue and weakness, while too much iron can be toxic to your organs. This test helps pinpoint the exact cause of your symptoms.

### Kidney Function Test
- **Price:** From £35
- **Category:** Blood Tests
- **Description:** Check how well your kidneys are filtering waste from your blood.
- **Details:** Your kidneys remove waste products and excess fluid from your body. A kidney function test measures levels of urea, creatinine, and certain dissolved salts (electrolytes) to determine if your kidneys are working properly.

### Cortisol Blood Test
- **Price:** From £35
- **Category:** Blood Tests
- **Description:** Measure your cortisol levels to evaluate adrenal gland function and stress response.
- **Details:** Cortisol is a hormone produced by your adrenal glands, often called the 'stress hormone'. Abnormal levels can indicate disorders of the adrenal glands or pituitary gland, or simply chronic high stress.

### PSA / Prostate Test
- **Price:** From £35
- **Category:** Blood Tests
- **Description:** A crucial screening test for men to check for prostate enlargement, inflammation, or prostate cancer risk.
- **Details:** Prostate-Specific Antigen (PSA) is a protein produced by both normal and malignant cells of the prostate gland. Elevated levels can indicate prostate cancer or noncancerous conditions such as prostatitis or an enlarged prostate.

### Liver Function Test
- **Price:** From £35
- **Category:** Blood Tests
- **Description:** Assess the health of your liver by measuring enzymes, proteins, and bilirubin in your blood.
- **Details:** The liver plays a vital role in detoxifying blood, producing proteins, and aiding digestion. A liver function test can help diagnose liver diseases, monitor treatment, or check for liver damage from medications.
